1. Set Clear Goals

Define specific, measurable, achievable, relevant, and time-bound (SMART) goals.

2. Create a Balanced Workout Routine

  • Cardio Exercises: Running, cycling, or swimming to improve heart health.
  • Strength Training: Weightlifting or bodyweight exercises to build muscle.
  • Flexibility & Mobility: Yoga or stretching to prevent injuries.
  • Rest & Recovery: Allow your body to heal and grow stronger.

3. Maintain a Healthy Diet

Follow these simple nutrition tips:

  • Eat whole foods: Incorporate fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, and whole grains.
  • Stay hydrated: Drink enough water to support metabolism and muscle function.
  • Balance macronutrients: Consume a mix of carbohydrates, proteins, and healthy fats.

4. Stay Consistent & Track Progress

Use fitness apps or journals to track workouts and diet, and adjust based on progress.

5. Stay Motivated & Have Fun

Find a workout you enjoy, mix up routines, and set rewards for reaching milestones.
