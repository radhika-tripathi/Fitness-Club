What is a High-Protein Diet?

A high-protein diet focuses on consuming foods rich in protein to promote muscle growth, weight loss, and overall health. Proteins are essential macronutrients that help repair tissues, produce enzymes and hormones, and support metabolic functions.

Benefits of a High-Protein Diet

  • Muscle Growth: Helps in building and repairing muscles.
  • Weight Management: Keeps you fuller for longer, reducing unnecessary cravings.
  • Metabolism Boost: Increases calorie burning and improves metabolic rate.
  • Improved Recovery: Aids in faster recovery after workouts.
